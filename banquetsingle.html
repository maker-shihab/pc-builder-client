About

Galleria Marchetti is a wedding venue located in scenic downtown Chicago, Illinois. Providing couples with an exquisite wedding backdrop, this elegant location is full of luxurious wonder and elegant charm. Complete with a range of services and a dedicated staff committed to exceptional service, it combines style and comfort for a magical wedding experience.

Facilities and Capacity

Drawing inspiration from classic Italian architecture, Galleria Marchetti has five indoor and outdoor spaces for couples to choose from. Depending on the size of your guest list, these rooms can be combined to create two separate venues or one larger space for greater numbers. Couples can select from the luminous opulence of the open-air La Pergola or the resplendent and refined interior of the Pavillion. With its European-inspired layout and large radiant windows, La Pergola can host between 160 and 300 guests. The expansive and eloquent Pavillion is designed for larger affairs, stunningly combining three separate spaces to accommodate between 300 and 600 people. Get-ready rooms are also available for you and your loved ones to prepare in peace and privacy.

Services Offered

As well as boasting captivating surroundings, this magnificent venue is home to a devoted and innovative service staff. Committed to professionalism, their primary aim is to make sure your day flows with minimal stress and maximum enjoyment. Whether you are looking to create a flawless seating arrangement or create the perfect color scheme, you will be in good hands at Galleria Marchetti.

Cuisine

With over 75 years of industry experience, the hardworking catering staff at the venue will aim to make your envisioned wedding meal a reality. They understand that your special day is a once-in-a-lifetime event and are more than happy to work with you to create the perfect culinary experience. From a sumptuous selection of hor dâ€™oeuvres to a completely customized menu for a 6-hour dinner reception, every detail can be covered. Linen, china, and glassware are all provided and are accompanied by a full-service staff to ensure that plates are kept full. The team also offers a premium selection of beverages including bar, wine, and spirits, as well as enhancements such as signature cocktails and champagne toasts. The culinary staff can also accommodate the following requests:

  • Pre-wedding tastings
  • Setup and cleanup
  • Buffet options
  • Food stations
  • Cake
  • Chocolate fountain
